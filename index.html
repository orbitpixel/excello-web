What We Do

Explore our PCB offerings across multiple board types and applications.

We serve a variety of sectors including aerospace, medical, automotive, high-end industrial, communications, and contract manufacturing. We manufacture printed circuit boards ranging from simple single-sided to heavy copper up to 16 oz, and complex multilayer and HDI boards up to 24 layers. Our aim is to provide flexibility and quality to meet our customer’s expectations. We provide flexibility and quality to ensure our customer’s expectations are exceeded.
